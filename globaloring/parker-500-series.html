Parker 5-XXX Series Size Chart / 派克5-XXX系列尺寸表

Parker Hannifin has established the 5-XXX series for a number of commonly used o-rings sizes which do not fall in the AS568 line of o-rings and are also not true “metric” sizes. The chart below lists all of these sizes with their corresponding dimensions in inches and millimeters for easy identification.
